WARNING: config.nackAddr is empty.
The binary will auto-detect from the egress interface. In containerized
deployments this may resolve to a SLAAC address that listeners' RETRY_ENDPOINTS
do not match — ACK/MISS responses will be silently filtered by the listeners.

operation. With networking.mode=multus, this should equal the address in
networking.multus.fabricIPv6 with the /64 mask stripped.
